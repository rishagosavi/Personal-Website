The Night She Disappeared

This is a book review on the book “The Night She Disappeared,” by April Henry. This book falls into the category and genre of mystery and suspense.

Plot

The setting of this book is in a small town located in Oregon. The main characters are Gabie, Drew and Kayla, the story is told by their individual divergent perspectives. All of them work shifts together at a pizza place, which delivers orders to their customers' front doors. Gabie drives a mini cooper, and one night Kayla asks Gabie to borrow her car. Undoubtedly, Gabie lends her car to Kayla. Little did she know that Kayla would never return again.

My Opinion

Overall I rate this book a 5/5 stars, for numerous reasons. The plot of this novel is deliberately skillfully planned and organized. Throughout the book, the reader will never become uninterested because the order of events will always keep them hooked.
